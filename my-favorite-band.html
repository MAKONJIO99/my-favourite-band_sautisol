Sauti Sol are a kenyan afro-pop This band formed in Nairobi, Kenya by vocalists Bien-Aimé Baraza, Willis Chimano and Savara Mudigi in 2005. Initially an a cappella group, guitarist Polycarp Otieno joined before they named themselves growing to be an award winning band not only in Kenya but in the conntinet (Africa)
